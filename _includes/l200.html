L200 Comedy

Comedy at L200 on Langstrasse! North of the tracks...

The doors open 7:30pm and we're usually done by 10pm latest.

L200 is a super cosy and friendly space and after the show, feel free to hangout and get to know the comedians and other people from the show!

Getting There

Bus 32 or a tram to Limmatplatz and a short walk - it's just opposite Lilly's - Directions to L200 on Google Maps here.

Drinks in the Ticket

L200 is not a bar so if you'd like drinks and want to support the show, we have higher priced tickets that include drinks, so we can get them for you in advance.
