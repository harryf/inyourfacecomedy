TITLE: Double Shot - English Comedy

New in town or just visiting? Double Shot is a one-night-only English stand-up comedy show at ROBIN's in Zürich, no Swiss German required.

This one is special. Martina and Harry, the two hosts you'd usually see running our weekly Comedy Brew open mic, step out from behind the microphone to perform a full two-person show of their own. No open-mic safety net this time, just two comedians who have spent years making Zürich laugh, doing it together for one night. The headline act is Martina, who is taking her first proper two-person show on the road around Switzerland, and tonight Harry is along for the ride.

Expect comedy that's quick, dark and honest, all performed in English. The crowd is the usual relaxed mix of internationals, expats and curious locals, so it's an easy night out whether you're travelling solo or bringing friends. Grab a drink, take a seat and settle in.

Doors open 7:30pm, the show starts at 8:00pm and wraps up around 10pm. Tickets are CHF 20, or CHF 15 if money is tight, and are best bought in advance online since it's one night only. Recommended 16+ for adult themes and language.

ROBIN's is at Zähringerstrasse 33, 8001 Zürich, two minutes from the Central tram stop and five from the main station.

Part of IN YOUR FACE Comedy, running English-language live shows in Zürich since 2018.
