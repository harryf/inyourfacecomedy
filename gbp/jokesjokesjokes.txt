New in town or just visiting? Jokes, Jokes, Jokes is a live English stand-up night, no Swiss German required.

This is the non-obvious comedy show. Hosted by Swiss-based comedian Łukasz Segiet, better known as Woocash, it leans into off-beat, unpredictable, vibe-driven comedy: the kind that gets laughs from places you didn't see coming.

Woocash is a favourite among fellow comedians for his distinctive approach, and each edition he invites a line-up of friends and local comics from Zürich and beyond to share the stage. Expect loose ideas, polished bits and unexpected jokes, all performed in English.

The crowd is a relaxed mix of internationals, expats and curious locals, so it's an easy night out whether you're travelling solo or just want somewhere low-key to spend an evening. Grab a drink, take a seat and settle in.

Doors open 7:00pm, show starts 7:30pm. Tickets are best bought in advance online, since space is limited. Recommended 16+ for adult themes and language.

Part of IN YOUR FACE Comedy, running English-language live shows in Zürich since 2018.
