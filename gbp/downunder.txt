New in town or just visiting? Down Under Comedy is a live English stand-up show every Tuesday in Zurich.

Australian comedian Chris Darwa hosts and brings his fast, unfiltered brand of comedy to the stage, joined each week by a rotating line-up of comics from Switzerland's English-language scene and from all around the world. Some are road-testing brand new jokes, others are sharpening sets they've toured, and the mix makes every Tuesday a different night. It's the kind of show where you turn up for a quiet drink and end up laughing far harder than you planned.

The crowd is a relaxed mix of internationals, expats and curious locals, so it's an easy night out whether you're travelling solo or want somewhere low-key to meet people. Grab a drink, take a seat and settle in.
Doors open 7:15pm, show starts 7:30pm. Tickets are best bought in advance online, since space is limited. Recommended 16+ for adult themes and language.

Part of IN YOUR FACE Comedy, running English-language live shows in Zürich since 2018.
