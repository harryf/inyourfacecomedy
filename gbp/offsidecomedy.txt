TITLE: Offside Comedy: English Stand-Up at ROBIN's Zürich

New in town or just visiting? Offside Comedy is a live English stand-up show every Tuesday in July, so you don't need a word of Swiss German to enjoy it.

While the rest of the world watches the football, hosts Chris Darwa and Harry, two regulars of Zürich's English comedy scene, put a microphone in the middle of the room instead of a trophy. Each week they bring two more comedians to the stage, and the audience decides who takes the night. The rules are loose, the comedy is sharp, and no football knowledge is required (or wanted).

It's a relaxed, friendly crowd of internationals, expats and curious locals, which makes it an easy evening if you're travelling solo or want to meet people. Grab a drink, take a seat and settle in.

Doors open at 7:15pm and the show runs from 7:30pm to 9:30pm, in good time for the last tram. Tickets are CHF 10, or CHF 5 for students, and are best bought in advance online.

ROBIN's is in the Niederdorf, two minutes from the Central tram stop and five from the main station. It's a café and bar by day that turns into a room for comedy at night.

Part of IN YOUR FACE Comedy, running English-language live shows in Zürich since 2018.
