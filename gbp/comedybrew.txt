Visiting Zürich or new in town? Comedy Brew is a live English stand-up comedy show every Thursday evening, so you don't need a word of Swiss German to enjoy it.

Each week, local talent from all around the world takes the stage: comedians originally from the UK, US, India, Ireland, Germany, Australia and beyond, all performing in English. It's an open mic format, which means experienced comedians road-testing brand new jokes alongside brave newcomers doing comedy for the very first time. With 10 or more acts on each show, every night is different: some gems, some flops, and the fun of watching comedy being born in real time.

It's a relaxed, friendly crowd of internationals, expats and curious locals, which makes it an easy way to spend an evening if you're travelling solo or want to meet people. Grab a drink, take a seat and settle in. Front row is safe: we chat with the crowd for laughs, not humiliation.

Doors open 7:30pm, show starts around 7:50pm with a short break, and we wrap up by 10pm, in plenty of time for the last tram. Tickets are best bought in advance online - checkout the website - but you can also get tickets on the door.

Recommended 16+ for adult themes and spicy language.

Running weekly since 2018 as part of IN YOUR FACE Comedy, Zürich's English stand-up comedy brand.
