<h2 class="lm__stage" id="stage-1"><span>1</span> Pick a show</h2>

Open the link and you land on a list of the shows coming up. Tap the one you are building a lineup for. That is the whole of Stage 1. The list keeps itself up to date, so the show you want is in there.

![Lineup Maker 2000, Stage 1: a list of upcoming shows to pick from.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/1_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

<h2 class="lm__stage" id="stage-2"><span>2</span> Whole show, or two halves</h2>

Now pick the shape of the night. A straight run, or a show split into two halves with a break in the middle. Pick whichever one matches your show. A night like Comedy Brew runs in two halves, so you would pick the one with the break.

![Lineup Maker 2000, Stage 2: choose a straight show or a show with two halves and a break.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/2_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

<h2 class="lm__stage" id="stage-3"><span>3</span> Add the comedians</h2>

Start typing a name in the search field, or just scroll the list. Tap a name on the left and that comedian gets added to your lineup. Tapped the wrong one? Tap the little **X** next to their name to drop them again. No harm done.

Next to each name sits a blue link with their handle in brackets. Not sure who someone is? Tap it. Their comedian page opens in a new tab, so you do not lose your place.

![Lineup Maker 2000, Stage 3: search for comedians, tap to add, tap the X to remove, blue link opens their page.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/3_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

### The comedian we have never heard of

Sometimes you book someone who is not on our website yet. No problem. Type their name into the search field and a little **+** shows up on the right. Tap it. They get added by name for the WhatsApp text and the share links, so nobody gets left off the running order.

The one thing a guest like this does not get is a spot on the flyer, because we only have art for the comedians on the site. Their name still travels everywhere else.

![Lineup Maker 2000, Stage 3: type a name that is not on the site and tap the plus to add them as a guest.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/4_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

<h2 class="lm__stage" id="stage-4"><span>4</span> Put them in order, then share it</h2>

Here is where the work pays off. The comedians show up in the order you added them. To shuffle the order, tap the little up or down arrow next to a name, or press and hold the three dots and drag them up or down the list.

Two more handles sit on each name. The little **microphone** makes that comedian the host of the show. The little **star** makes them a headliner, which gives them a bigger spot on the share links and the flyer. A night like Comedy Brew has no headliner, so you would skip the star. A show with one big name and some openers is where the star earns its keep.

![Lineup Maker 2000, Stage 4: reorder with arrows or drag, microphone sets the host, star sets a headliner.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/5_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

<div class="lm__note" markdown="0">
<p>You can step back at any time and Lineup Maker 2000 keeps what you had. Go back, add more comedians, swap one out, then come forward again. Nothing is lost. The tool has a longer memory than you do.</p>
</div>

### Where does the break go?

If you picked the two-halves show, the **BREAK** starts at the bottom of the list. Drag it up to the right spot between the comedians. Wherever you drop it is where it shows up when you copy the running order. The next screenshot shows the copy.

![Lineup Maker 2000, Stage 4: drag the BREAK up the list to set where the interval falls.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/6_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

<p class="lm__q">How do I get this into WhatsApp?</p>

Glad you asked, because this is the part everyone wants. Tap the copy button for the running order and it lands on your clipboard, ready to paste straight into the group chat.

![Lineup Maker 2000, Stage 4: copy the running order to your clipboard for WhatsApp.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/7_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

### The three links and the flyer

Scroll down a touch and you get three links to copy, plus a red button for the flyer.

![Lineup Maker 2000, Stage 4: the show promo link, the thank-you link, the save-for-later link, and the red flyer button.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/8_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

- **Show promo link.** Share this before the show, on an Instagram story or anywhere. It sends people to a page that shows the lineup, names the host, and lets them buy a ticket. Not sure what it shows? Tap the yellow link to see it first.
- **Thank-you link.** Send this after the show, by email or DM, to the crowd or to the comedians who turned up.
- **Save lineup for later.** This is the smart one. The whole lineup lives inside this link. Get pulled away halfway through? Copy it and come back later, right where you stopped. Or hand it to another comedian and let them finish the lineup for you. The link is the save file.
- **Make a flyer** (the red button) builds an image for Instagram stories and posts.

### The flyer

The flyer is built from the show art plus the comedians listed on the IN YOUR FACE website. Switch between story shape and post shape with the toggle, depending on where it is going.

![Lineup Maker 2000, Stage 4: the generated flyer, switchable between Instagram story and post shape.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/9_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

### Getting the flyer onto Instagram

Tap the download button to save the image. One thing to know on an iPhone: it lands in your **Files** app, not your **Photos** app.

![Lineup Maker 2000, Stage 4: the download button saves the flyer as a PNG.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/10_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

So open the Files app, tap the flyer, and in the preview tap **Save Image** to send it across to Photos. Now it is ready to upload to Instagram.

![On iPhone: open the flyer in the Files app preview and tap Save Image to add it to Photos.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/11_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

### Tag everyone in one go

Below the download button is a button that copies all the Instagram handles of the comedians on the flyer. Paste them into a story or a post to tag the whole lineup at once.

![Lineup Maker 2000, Stage 4: copy every comedian's Instagram handle in one tap.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/12_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}

You get a block like this:

```
@martinadoescomedy 
@andrea.comedy 
@jackrobertscomedy 
@chrisdarwa 
@laugh_at_domenico 
@harryf.cks 

```

<p class="lm__q">Why is nobody actually tagged?</p>

Here is the trick that trips everyone up. When you paste the handles into Instagram, they are not tags yet. A handle is only a real tag once Instagram **underlines** it. To make that happen, tap at the end of a name, where there is a single space after the handle, and delete that space. Instagram then underlines the name and turns it into a real tag.

Work down the list, underline each one, then drag the names off the edge of the screen. They stay tagged, your story stays clean.

![On Instagram: delete the trailing space after each handle so it underlines, which means it is now a real tag.](/assets/img/lineup-manual/13_lineup_maker_2000.png){:.lm__shot}
