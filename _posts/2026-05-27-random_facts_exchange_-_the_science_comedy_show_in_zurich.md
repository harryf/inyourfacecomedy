Random Facts Exchange is what happens when you give nerds a microphone instead of telling them to stop.

![Random Facts Exchange Zurich](/assets/img/uploads/random_facts_exchange.webp "Random Facts Exchange Zurich")

It's a science comedy show where real humans get up and present whatever weird thing they're obsessed with. The mating habits of Swiss alpine ants. Why your toaster has feelings. The geopolitics of pineapple. How to mathematically prove your ex was wrong. Physics, history, psychology, anything goes, as long as it's interesting and at least slightly funny.

Think TED Talk, but the speaker is your friend who fell into a Wikipedia hole at 2am and never came back. Inspired by An Evening of Unnecessary Detail, the Ig Nobel Prizes, and the entire science YouTube rabbit hole.

Come for the facts. Stay because you're trapped between two strangers and the show hasn't ended yet.
