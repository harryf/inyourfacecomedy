# Nicholas De Santo - Non-Neutral Comedy - Live in Zurich

![Nicholas De Santo - Non-Neutral Comedy - Live in Zurich](/assets/img/uploads/nicholas_de_santo_portrait_nolink.jpeg "Nicholas De Santo - Non-Neutral Comedy - Live in Zurich")

Fresh from his tour of Ohio & Michigan (USA), Nicholas De Santo makes his debut in Switzerland, for a night of unique conservative comedy!

Nicholas De Santo is an award winning Italian-born comic based in London, England.

One of a handful of conservative stand-up comedians in the UK, he challenges the leftist, mainstream agenda with counter-culture comedy and political satire.

Nicholas De Santo has over [100K subscribers on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@AngloItalianComedy) and his routines have been viewed more than 10 million times. In 2019, he was UK's New Act of the Year (NATYS) finalist at Hackney Empire Theatre, London.

He has performed in North America, Berlin, Brussels and frequently appears as a comic and commentator on GB News national TV channel.

- "One of the best unwoke comics to watch" – The Spectator
- "An unapologetic right-winger" – Evening Standard

**Doors Open 19:30, Show Begins 20:00**
